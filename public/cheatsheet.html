In that view you can check the voice command defined in the application. The command, in that case, are all of them defined in spanish.

# Command Description
1 saluda Say hello to the platform
2 duerme Don't obey commands
3 despierta Resume obeying commands
4 menú *
  • "inicio": go to home
  • "cuadro de mandos": go to dashboard view
  • "sensores": go to sensors view
  • "aplicaciones": go to apps view
  • "jardín": go to garden view
  • "configuración": go to configuration section
5 lanzar *
  • "modo zen": open zen selector popup
  • "reproductor": open music player
6 reproducir música Play music
7 siguiente canción Next player
8 parar música Stop music
9 accionar lámpara Switch lamp state
10 accionar ventilador Switch fun state
11 cerrar Close popup
